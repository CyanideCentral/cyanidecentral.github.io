About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am Yiran Li, a final-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research focuses on developing efficient algorithms for clustering and embedding complex networks. I am also interested in graph learning and AI4science.

Before my PhD, I completed my Bachelor's degree in software engineering at Nanjing University in 2019.

Research Interests
