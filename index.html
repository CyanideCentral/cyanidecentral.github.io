About Me

I am Yiran Li, a postdoctoral fellow at ECE, University of Toronto, working with Prof. Hans-Arno Jacobsen. My research centers on designing efficient and scalable algorithms for clustering and embedding complex networks. I am also deeply interested in graph learning and the intersection of artificial intelligence with scientific discovery (AI4Science).

I got my PhD from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2025, under the supervision of Prof. Jieming Shi. Before my PhD, I completed my Bachelor's degree in software engineering at Nanjing University in 2019.
